Mobility solutions: Ola offers mobility solutions such as bikes, auto-rickshaws, metered taxis, and cabs. Social welfare: Ola's social welfare arm, the Ola Foundation, works to ensure women have access to resources and opportunities. Electric vehicles: Ola Electric is Ola's electric-vehicle arm. Fleet management: Ola Fleet Technologies is Ola's fleet management business. Public transportation ticketing: Ridlr is Ola's public transportation ticketing app. Financial services: Ola Financial Services offers micro-insurance products and a range of payment solutions. Food brands: Ola delivers food brands directly to customers from its network of dark kitchens.
